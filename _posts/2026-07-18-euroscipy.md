During the <a href="https://euroscipy.org/">EuroSciPy 2026 conference</a> at AGH in Kraków,
  <b>Gracjan Adamus</b> will present a talk in the Computational Tools and Scientific Python Infrastructure
  track titled 
  "<em><a href="https://euroscipy.org/schedule/#session/TENZMQ/">Engineering Fortran-to-Python Bindings in C++ with <cite>nanobind[_json]</cite> and <cite>cibuildwheel</cite></a></em>";
  <b>Sylwester Arabas</b> and <b>Kamil Kania</b> will present a tutorial session 
  on <a href="https://euroscipy.org/schedule/#session/8TC88B/">Gluing SciPy, Numba, and Pint to Bridge High Performance with Maintainability</a>; 
  and <b>Agnieszka Arabas</b> will serve as a volunteer.
